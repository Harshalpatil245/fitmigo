🏃‍♂️ Who We Are

FitMigo is more than fitness—it's friendship, motivation, and community. We are building a space where people connect over shared wellness goals, support each other, and grow stronger together.

Whether you're taking your first step or restarting your journey, FitMigo is your fitness family in the making.

🌱 Our Core Programs

Tailored wellness solutions for every stage of your fitness journey

🥗 Basic Nutrition Guidance

  • Practical tips to eat clean and smart
  • Portion control, hydration, and energy-boosting habits
  • Weekly advice from certified experts

🍽️ Personalized Nutrition Plans

  • Tailored meal plans based on your goals (fat loss, energy, lifestyle)
  • Indian & global diet styles supported
  • Direct consultation with experienced nutritionists

🔥 Fat Loss Programs

  • Beginner to intermediate home/workout routines
  • Pair with your Migo (buddy) for motivation
  • Simple tracker templates and habit logs

🧒 Teenage Health Plans

  • Fitness and food strategies for growing teens
  • Focus on strength, posture, and confidence
  • Parental guidance and safe, friendly routines

🩺 Diabetic Wellness Plans

  • Customized low-sugar meal formats
  • Light, safe exercise recommendations
  • Regular wellness check-in (manual, not digital yet)

👣 Reflexology & Wellness

  • Weekly online sessions
  • Stress, sleep, and hormonal balance support
  • Available online and through video guidance

Meet the Founder

The vision behind FitMigo's community-first approach

Miss Aditi Mukherjee

Certified Fitness Coach & Community Builder

Background & Vision: Miss Aditi Mukherjee envisioned FitMigo after seeing how support and camaraderie transformed her own fitness journey. Instead of solo workouts and quick fixes, she believes fitness thrives in friendship and shared goals.

Credentials & Experience:

  • Certified Nutrition Consultant
  • 5+ years of helping individuals find balance and well-being
  • Facilitator for teens, beginners, and those managing health conditions

"To bring fitness back to its roots—as a shared experience built on trust, care, and mutual encouragement."
